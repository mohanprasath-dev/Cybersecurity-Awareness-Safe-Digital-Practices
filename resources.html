Trusted External Resources

Authoritative sources and organizations dedicated to cybersecurity awareness.

CISA (Cybersecurity & Infrastructure Security Agency)

Official US government resource for cybersecurity guidance, alerts, and resources for individuals and organizations.

NIST Cybersecurity Framework

Industry-standard framework for managing cybersecurity risk, widely adopted by organizations worldwide.

SANS Security Awareness

World-renowned security training organization offering resources, research, and educational materials.

Have I Been Pwned

Free service to check if your email or passwords have been compromised in known data breaches.

Electronic Frontier Foundation

Digital rights organization providing privacy tools, guidance, and advocacy for online security and freedom.
